# 4.3.2 LENTGH OF THE WRITTEN PRODUCT

The required length of the written product varies with the number of tasks set. When only one writing task is assigned (and this can only be a free writing task), the required length should approximate the number of words indicated in the table below:

## Format/Genre of Writing

- formal/informal letters
- film/book reviews
- articles
- e-mails
- reports
- paragraphs
- multi-paragraph essays
- biographies

## Types of Writing

- narrative
- descriptive
- argumentative
- expository
- persuasive
