Datasets — what exists & what you must build
✓ Ready on Hugging Face
meta-math/MetaMathQA
395K math QA pairs with chain-of-thought. K-12 to competition level. Use for fine-tuning math reasoning.
SciInstruct (Tsinghua)
254K science instructions (physics, chemistry, biology) with self-verified reasoning steps. Strong for Bac science.
ArabicaQA (SIGIR 2024)
Comprehensive Arabic QA — machine reading comprehension + open domain. Good for Arabic subject Q&A.
wiki_qa_ar (HuggingFace)
Arabic Wikipedia QA pairs. Good for general knowledge (history, geography, science) in Arabic.
ajibawa-2023/Maths-Grade-School
Grade school math word problems with full solutions. Perfect for primary + collège levels.
HuggingFaceH4/aime_2024
High school math competition problems. Use for Bac Maths / SM track evaluation and challenge sets.
⚠ Must be built / scraped by you
Moroccan Bac national exams corpus
Scrape from schoolfibers.com, chtoukaphysique.com, 9rayti.com, sigmaths.net — all have PDFs of past exams with corrections (2003–2025). Convert to QA pairs.
Cadre de référence (frame of reference)
Official MEN documents define what's in each exam. Download from men.gov.ma for each track (SM, PC, SVT, SGC, lettres). Convert to structured curriculum maps.
Manhaj.ma / 9rayti.com course content
Rich Moroccan pedagogical content per subject and level. Can be scraped into (course → summary → exercise → answer) structured datasets.
Collège & primaire level content
Almost no HuggingFace dataset covers Moroccan collège/primaire. You'll need to create this from textbooks, tawjihnet.net, or Manhaj.
No ready-made Moroccan school curriculum dataset exists on Hugging Face. This is both the biggest gap and your biggest competitive advantage if you build it.
Recommended build strategy
1
Start with Qwen2.5-VL-7B as your PDF parser
Use it on Kaggle/Colab to extract Moroccan exam PDFs into structured text. This becomes your raw dataset.
2
Fine-tune DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-14B
Use LoRA (via Unsloth library) on Moroccan exam QA pairs. This gives you a reasoning model that knows the Moroccan curriculum.
3
Add RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Index your course PDFs with a vector database. The model retrieves the right cours before answering — no hallucination about Moroccan-specific content.
4
Build the Moroccan curriculum dataset in parallel
Scrape + clean national exam PDFs → convert to (question, reasoning steps, answer) format → push to HuggingFace. This is your most valuable asset.
