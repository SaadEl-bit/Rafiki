Immediate Priorities: AI Models & Datasets

Overview of the specific technological requirements to power the platform, focusing on Moroccan curriculum data and specialized logic solvers.

Required AI Models

  • ► PDF Analysis & OCR: Ability to ingest user-uploaded courses, exercises, and scanned exams (French/Arabic/Math symbols).
  • ► Logic Resolution: Specialized models for Math, Physics, and Science step-by-step problem solving.
  • ► Multilingual NLP: Strong capabilities in Formal Arabic (فصحى), French, and English for curriculum comprehension.

Targeted Datasets

  • ► Moroccan Curriculum: Complete courses, exercises, and exams (Primary → High School).
  • ► Pedagogical Frameworks: (الإطار المرجعي + البيداغوجية) to ensure AI alignment with official standards.
  • ► Domain Knowledge: Moroccan history, geography, and Islamic education specifics.
